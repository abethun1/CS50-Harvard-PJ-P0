Endomorph

An ectomorph is a typical skinny guy. Ecto’s have a light build with small joints and lean muscle. Usually ectomorph’s have long thin limbs with stringy muscles.

Facts about Diet

Ectomorph have a less chance of gaining excess fat. They should have their carbohydrates like pasta, bananas, beans, rice etc. Including carbohydrates in their diet plans is a very important thing to do. They should also load themselves with protein. Examples of protein foods are eggs, seeds, nuts, tuna, chicken. After their workout, having a protein shake would be great.
